Welcome to TabShift Pro!

Use the following keyboard shortcuts to rearrange tabs:

  • Move Left: Shift+Alt+Left (Windows), Command+MacCtrl+Left (Mac)
  • Move Right: Shift+Alt+Right (Windows), Command+MacCtrl+Right (Mac)
  • Move to Front: Shift+Alt+Up (Windows), Command+MacCtrl+Up (Mac)
  • Move to Back: Shift+Alt+Down (Windows), Command+MacCtrl+Down (Mac)

For more information, visit Mohammad Wasim Tikki's GitHub.
